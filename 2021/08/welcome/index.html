Welcome

Welcome to the Blog of David Smith

I thought the time was right to start to give back to the technology community with all that I have learned in my career in technology. This blog will contain mostly things I am working on currently. If I come across something interesting that I have already created I will add that as well.

I will provide content about SCOM, Remedy Incidents from SCOM, Powershell scripts, vRealize Automation, REST API’s and maybe some C# goodies. I am always open to suggested content as well. I have no idea what others are interested in or what they may be looking to build.

I used Tranquilpeek site theme from Hugo for my Github page. I have a post with an overview of how I installed/setup Hugo and Github. Take a look at that post “Github Hugo Setup” if interested. For more info about Tranquilpeak theme take a look at the link below.

  • https://github.com/kakawait/hugo-tranquilpeak-theme/blob/master/docs/user.md
